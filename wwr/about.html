Our History

Since our founding in 1995, Rapids Rafting has offered exciting white water rafting adventures for thrill-seekers across the country. Our expert guides and focus on safety have earned us a reputation for unforgettable river journeys.

What started as a local passion project has grown into a nationally recognized adventure company. We love the outdoors, and we love sharing it with others!
