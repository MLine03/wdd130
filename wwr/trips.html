Our Rafting Trips

Whether you're looking for a fun family adventure or an adrenaline-pumping thrill ride, Whitewater Rafting Adventures has the trip for you. Explore our most popular options below.

Half-Day Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families with kids. This short but exciting trip offers a taste of rafting without taking up your entire day.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Minimum Age: 6 years old

Full-Day Expedition

Our most popular trip! Experience the thrill of whitewater all day with a break for a riverside lunch included.

  • Duration: 6–7 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Minimum Age: 12 years old

Extreme Whitewater Challenge

For serious thrill-seekers only. Navigate advanced Class IV+ rapids under the guidance of our expert guides.

  • Duration: 4–5 hours
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Minimum Age: 18 years old
